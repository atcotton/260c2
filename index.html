About this Project

Now, I'm sure you're wondering how this isn't just our weather api project thing.

Long story short: it is.

You'll notice that you never passed it a city when you started though. Your location was deduced via your ip address. That ip address was passed over to ip-api.com and they gave me your estimated location, which was then fed to our friends over at OpenWeatherMap.com.
And let me tell you does that make how you structure your promises important.


Limitations

There is a none zero chance that your location was pulled incorretly though. IP addresses don't always translate nicely to physical locations.
